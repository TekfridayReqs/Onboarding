    01 Stage One

    Know Your Company

    Who we are. TekFriday Processing Solution is a fintech company whose mission is to build innovative solutions that help financial services companies based in the US grow and compete in this fast-paced world.

    Our Origin Story

    In an overcrowded alternative-lending market where conformity was the norm, three individuals set out to build a disruptive force — to sway away from that conformity and become a pioneer. Anil, Srinath and Mridula saw a gap in the industry and, using their collective ideation and ingenuity, built solutions that help alternative-lending entities become leaders in their own segment. We started operations in 2013 with fewer than 20 members; today we have grown into a team of more than 200. In 2019, TekFriday was acquired by Advance Financials and became a captive delivery centre.

    What We Do

    We have built a range of solutions. These are the core services we deliver:

    Portfolio ManagementData AnalyticsTransaction Processing Digital MarketingApplication DevelopmentProduction & Maintenance

    How We're Different

    We are a lean team that works in an agile set-up, thriving in fast-paced environments to help deliver every one of the services above with speed and focus.

    Our Goal

    To always look to innovate and build solutions that help us grow, while aligning with our business strategy — redefining financial solutions with cutting-edge technology.

    Our Values

    Innovative

    We look at a problem or process and seek better ways to work, while creating value to help grow the business. This isn't about superficially using words like "automation" or "AI" — it's about real changes and optimizations that make a process work better or create more value.

    Adaptive

    Embrace change while remaining agile. Because we always operate in a lean and agile way, we don't expect employees to stay rigid about "roles and responsibilities" — we expect everyone to embrace change and work toward a common, collective goal.

    Impact

    Focus on business outcomes that create meaningful value while aligning with company strategy. Everyone has a role to play in getting us where we want to be, so we want you to reframe how your work and actions create impact on the business.

    Transparency & Compliance

    Be respectful, honest and communicate openly. Unlike traditional organizations, we don't work in a mechanistic or flat structure — we work in a matrix model where we collaborate across departments to drive business excellence. That means keeping egos aside, working as a collective unit, and staying open to feedback.

    02 Stage Two

    Know Your Culture

    "The only way to do great work is to love what you do."— Steve Jobs

    Our work culture is defined by six everyday behaviours. Together they shape how decisions are made, how we collaborate, and how success is achieved.

    Ownership

    Whatever task is allotted to you, you are the owner — the sole torchbearer responsible for completing it to the best of your ability.

    Accountability

    Completing a task is admirable, but are you giving the best possible outcome? Always ask yourself: is this my best work?

    Collaboration

    Because we work in a matrix hierarchy, we expect open channels of communication and collective work toward the common goal — while avoiding bureaucratic red tape. Work should be seamless, without the hurdles of unwarranted approvals.

    Continuous Learning

    We encourage everyone to learn skills or technologies that improve our systems or approach. Every employee has the potential to exceed today's limits and become an asset — and this isn't restricted to your current work; it can be anything you're interested in, as long as we already use it or it would help our systems.

    Strategy Focus

    We are a captive delivery unit for a US lending company, so our strategy and goals align with our parent company's: to be the best in the industry while remaining a disruptive innovator. Every decision and action should align with this notion.

    Respect

    We foster an inclusive, collaborative ecosystem that values every insight and idea. There are no bad ideas — though we always check whether an idea helps us achieve our goals. We come from different backgrounds and aspirations, and together we respect each other's opinions, feedback and ways of life. Whoever you are, if you believe in our values and respect one another, you'll fare well here.

    03 Stage Three

    Know Your Team

    Because we work in a matrix structure, understanding how the team connects — and what's expected of you — is key to moving fast without friction.

    Team Orientation

    Here you'll get to know the organization and reporting structure:

    • Where you sit within the organization chart.
    • What a line manager and a functional manager are, and how they differ.
    • Who your reporting manager is — and who else can assign you work apart from them.
    • Who your key stakeholders are: the important members you'll work and collaborate with.
    • Your cross-functional dependencies — the stakeholders and departments you'll work with.

    Team Expectations

    Four things we count on from everyone:

    • Communication — communicate, be proactive, and update regularly.
    • Collaboration — support one another and share knowledge or skills that help streamline the work or process.
    • Problem Solving — focus on solutions, not problems. Issues will arise in any application; we always look at how to make sure that kind of problem never comes up again.
    • Professionalism — integrity in personal and professional conduct; always be reliable and respect each other.

    9 HR Policies

    Code of Conduct

    This policy outlines the standards of professional behaviour, integrity and ethical conduct that all employees must uphold while representing the Company.

    Any breach of this policy is treated seriously and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
    1.1.1

    No Smoking Zone

    All company premises are non-smoking zones. Employees may not use tobacco products — smoking or chewing — within these areas.

    1.1.2

    No Substance Abuse

    A zero-tolerance policy applies. Employees may not use, possess, distribute, or be under the influence of illegal drugs, alcohol or intoxicants on premises, during work hours, or while representing the Company externally.

    1.1.3

    Honesty & Ethical Behaviour

    Uphold the highest standards of honesty, integrity and ethics. Moonlighting: no external employment that conflicts with your responsibilities — it must be disclosed and approved by Management. Anti-corruption: no bribery, kickbacks, or corrupt practices in any dealings.

    1.1.4

    Workplace Behaviour

    Abusive, offensive or inappropriate language is strictly prohibited. Physical or verbal altercations are serious violations and may lead to termination. Maintain a respectful, professional demeanour at all times.

    1.1.5

    Work Assignments

    Refusing to perform assigned duties without a valid reason is a breach of the Code of Conduct and may result in disciplinary action.

    1.1.6

    Confidentiality

    Protect the Company's intellectual property, client interests and competitive position. Do not use, copy, share or distribute company-owned inventions, designs, software or proprietary processes without prior authorization.

    1.1.7

    Company Assets

    Laptops, vehicles, internet, telephones, mobiles and communication tools must be used responsibly and strictly for official business. Misuse is a policy violation.

    1.1.8

    Data Protection & Privacy

    Handle personal and confidential data with care: access and share it strictly for official purposes; store and transmit it securely; maintain confidentiality; and report any suspected breach immediately.

    1.1.9

    Disciplinary Measures

    Proportionate to the violation: warnings (verbal/written), suspension (7 days, 10 days, 1 month or 3 months without pay), revocation of privileges, demotion (rare), or termination. All actions follow due process and management approval.
