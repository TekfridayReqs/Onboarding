    01 Stage One

    Know Your Company

    Who we are. TekFriday Processing Solution is a fintech company. Our mission is to build innovative solutions that help financial services companies across the US grow and compete in a fast-moving world.

    Our Story

    In an overcrowded alternative-lending market where conformity was the norm, three people set out to be a disruptive force — and a pioneer. Anil, Srinath and Mridula saw a gap in the industry and combined their ideas and ingenuity to build solutions that help alternative-lending companies lead their own segments. We began operations in 2013 with fewer than 20 people; today we are a team of more than 200.

    What We Do
    Portfolio Management Data Analytics Transaction Processing Digital Marketing Application Development Production & Maintenance

    How We're Different

    We are a lean team working in an agile setup. Our people thrive in fast-paced environments, which lets us deliver every one of these services with speed and focus.

    Our Goal

    To keep innovating and building solutions that help us grow — always aligned with our business strategy — by redefining financial solutions with cutting-edge technology.

    Our Values

    Innovation

    Look at every problem or process and find a better way to work — real improvements that create value, not just buzzwords like "automation" or "AI".

    Adaptability

    Embrace change and stay agile. Rather than staying rigid about roles, we adapt and pull together toward a common, collective goal.

    Impact

    Focus on business outcomes that create meaningful value and align with company strategy. Everyone has a role in getting us where we want to be.

    Transparency & Compliance

    Be respectful, honest and open. We work in a matrix structure, collaborating across departments — egos aside and open to feedback.

    02 Stage Two

    Know Your Culture

    "The only way to do great work is to love what you do."— Steve Jobs

    Our culture shows up in six everyday behaviours:

    Ownership

    The task assigned to you is yours. You are its sole torchbearer — see it through to the best of your ability.

    Accountability

    Finishing a task is admirable — but is it your best work? Always aim for the best possible outcome.

    Collaboration

    In our matrix structure we keep communication open and work toward the common goal — without red tape or unnecessary approvals. Work should flow seamlessly.

    Continuous Learning

    Learn skills and technologies that improve how we work. Every employee can grow beyond today's limits — and learning isn't limited to your current role.

    Strategy Focus

    As a captive delivery centre for a US lending company, our goals align with our parent's: to lead the industry while staying a disruptive innovator.

    Respect

    An inclusive ecosystem that values every insight. There are no bad ideas. Whoever you are, if you live our values and respect one another, you'll thrive here.

    03 Stage Three

    Know Your Team

    We work in a matrix structure, so knowing how the team connects is key to moving fast without friction.

    Team Orientation

    Here you'll learn the organization and reporting structure:

    • Where you sit in the organization chart.
    • The difference between your line manager and your functional manager.
    • Who your reporting manager is — and who else can assign you work.
    • Who your key stakeholders are: the people you'll work and collaborate with most.
    • Your cross-functional dependencies — the stakeholders and departments you'll work with.

    Team Expectations

    Four things we count on from everyone:

    • Communication — communicate proactively and share regular updates.
    • Collaboration — support one another and share knowledge and skills that make work flow better.
    • Problem Solving — focus on solutions, not problems; when an issue arises, find a way to stop it recurring.
    • Professionalism — act with integrity, be reliable, and respect each other.

    06 Stage Six

    Your 30 · 60 · 90 Day Journey

    Your probation period is a two-way fit check: our chance to see whether our culture works for you, and yours to do the same — all while you learn our business, processes and scope of work. We believe the move from candidate to employee is the most important part of your experience, so we make sure the right tools, knowledge and people are there for you from day one.

    First 30 Days

    🎓 Learn

    • Company & culture
    • Team structure & reporting
    • Role & process understanding
    • Tools & systems
    Outcome: a strong understanding of the organization and your role.
    Days 31 – 60

    🚀 Contribute

    • Independent task execution
    • Team collaboration
    • Process adherence
    • Skill development
    Outcome: the ability to contribute effectively with limited supervision.
    Days 61 – 90

    🏆 Own

    • Full task ownership
    • Performance delivery
    • Stakeholder management
    Outcome: consistent delivery and alignment with our culture.
