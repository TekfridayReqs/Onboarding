    Welcome to TekFriday

    01 Stage One

    Know Your Company

    TekFriday Processing Solution is a fintech company on a mission to build innovative solutions that help financial services companies in the US grow and compete in a fast-moving world. Founded in 2013 by Anil, Srinath & Mridula with fewer than 20 people, we have grown into a 200+ member team. In 2019 we were acquired by Advance Financials and became its captive delivery centre.

    What We Do
    Portfolio Management Data Analytics Transaction Processing Digital Marketing Application Development Production & Maintenance

    Our Vision

    Delivering innovative & impactful fintech solutions that create sustainable value for our customers and stakeholders.

    Our Mission

    To solve complex business challenges through technology and operational excellence — staying ahead of competitors by redefining financial solutions with cutting-edge technology.

    How We're Different & Our Core Values

    We're a lean team that works in an agile setup, moving quickly to deliver across every service. Four values guide how we do it:

    Innovative

    Find better ways to work and create real value — meaningful change, not buzzwords.

    Adaptive

    Embrace change and stay agile. Move toward the collective goal, not rigid roles.

    Impact

    Focus on outcomes that create meaningful value and align with company strategy.

    Transparency

    Be respectful, honest and open. Collaborate across functions, egos aside.

    02 Stage Two

    Know Our Culture

    "Culture defines how decisions are made, how teams collaborate, and how success is achieved."

    Ownership

    The task assigned to you is yours to carry. Take responsibility for commitments and outcomes.

    Accountability

    Completing a task is good — delivering your best work is the standard. Own the result.

    Collaboration

    Work openly across functions toward the common goal — without bureaucratic red tape.

    Continuous Learning

    Stay curious. Learn skills and tech that improve our systems and grow your potential.

    Strategy Focus

    Align every decision with our parent company's strategy — to lead and disrupt our industry.

    Respect

    An inclusive ecosystem where every idea is valued. Respect each other's opinions and feedback.

    03 Stage Three

    Know Your Team

    We work in a matrix model — so knowing how the team connects is key to moving fast without friction.

    Team Orientation

    • Organizational structure & reporting lines — where you sit, and who your line and functional managers are.
    • Team objectives and priorities for the period ahead.
    • Key stakeholders — the people and departments you'll work and collaborate with.
    • Cross-functional dependencies that connect your work to others.

    Team Expectations

    • Communication — be proactive, give regular updates, and escalate early.
    • Collaboration — support one another and share knowledge openly.
    • Problem Solving — focus on solutions, and prevent problems from recurring.
    • Professionalism — act with integrity, be reliable, and respect each other.

    06 Stage Six

    Your 30 · 60 · 90 Day Journey

    Probation is a two-way fit check — for us to support your learning, and for you to settle into our business, people and work.

    First 30 Days

    🎓 Learn

    • Company & culture overview
    • Team structure & reporting
    • Role & process understanding
    • Tools & systems onboarding
    Outcome: a strong understanding of the organization & role expectations.
    Days 31 – 60

    🚀 Contribute

    • Independent task execution
    • Team collaboration
    • Process adherence
    • Skill development
    Outcome: ability to contribute effectively with limited supervision.
    Days 61 – 90

    🏆 Own

    • Full ownership of deliverables
    • Performance delivery
    • Stakeholder management
    Outcome: consistent delivery and alignment with company culture.
